Can You See Me?

3D Motion

Can You See Me is an immersive 3D animation exploring the unsettling experience of sleep paralysis, designed to be viewed in Auckland's StarDome and had three very sucessful showings. Through layered sound effects and a carefully crafted suspense, it draws viewers into the intense helplessness and fear that define this state.
